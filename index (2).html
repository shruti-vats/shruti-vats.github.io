How I actually work

⚡ Zapier Candidate Onboarding → Document Verification Flow Live · 847 runs

⚡ Click any step below to expand full details

📝
Trigger · Google Forms
New Onboarding Form Submitted
Candidate submits onboarding form with personal details, emergency contacts, and document list. Zap fires instantly on submission. Fields mapped: Name, Role, Joining Date, Manager, Department.
App: Google Forms · Event: New Form Response · Instant trigger
📧
Action 1 · Gmail
Send Document Request Email to Candidate
Auto-sends personalised email listing required documents (Aadhaar, PAN, degree certificates, previous offer letters). Candidate name and joining date pulled dynamically from form data. Template: doc-request-v3.
Avg open rate: 94% · Send time: <30 seconds after form submission
🔀
Filter · Condition Check
Has Candidate Uploaded Documents? (Check after 48hrs)
Checks Google Drive folder for candidate documents after 48 hours. If documents found → runs acknowledgement branch. If not → runs single reminder branch. Candidates who remain unresponsive after 5 days are auto-flagged and removed from active pipeline.
Condition: Drive folder contains files · Wait: 48 hrs
✅
Action 2A · On Upload
Acknowledgement Sent + Tracker Updated + HR Notified
Confirmation email sent to candidate. Candidate row in tracker auto-updated to "Docs Verified". HR notified on Slack with candidate name and joining date. Calendar invite for Day 1 auto-created.
Outcome: Candidate advances to Active Onboarding · Zero manual steps
⚠️
Action 2B · No Upload
One Automated Reminder + Pipeline Cleanup
Single follow-up email sent. If still no response after 5 days → candidate flagged "Unresponsive" in tracker → removed from active onboarding pipeline → HR notified to reassess. No manual chasing. Ever.
Outcome: Clean pipeline · Zero ghost candidates · Zero HR time spent
