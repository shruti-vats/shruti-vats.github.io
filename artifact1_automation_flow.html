Portfolio Artifact · Workflow Design

Candidate Onboarding
Automation Flow

Built at Opt2Deal E-commerce Pvt. Ltd. using Zapier. Replaced fully manual follow-up for every new hire — triggered by form submission, self-running through document verification and candidate pipeline management.

Tool: Zapier
Context: Startup · First HR Hire
Scope: 50+ hires automated
Status: Production · 2022–2023
1
Trigger
Candidate Submits Onboarding Form
Candidate fills out the onboarding form — name, role, joining date, and contact details. This submission is the single trigger that starts the entire automated pipeline.
Zapier · Form Integration (Google Forms / Typeform)
2
Automated
Document Request Email Sent Instantly
On form submission, Zapier immediately triggers a personalised email listing all documents required for verification: ID proof, address proof, educational certificates, previous employment documents, and PAN/Aadhar.
Zapier · Gmail / SMTP Action
3
Decision Point
Did the Candidate Submit Documents?
After a defined window (e.g. 48–72 hours), Zapier checks whether documents have been received. This branches the flow into two separate paths.
Zapier · Filter / Delay Step
✓ Yes — Documents Submitted
Acknowledgement Email Sent
Candidate receives a confirmation email: documents received, verification in progress, team will revert within X working days.
Zapier · Gmail Action
✗ No — No Submission
One Reminder Email Sent
Single automated reminder sent after the deadline. No manual chase needed. If documents are still not submitted after reminder window, candidature is automatically removed from pipeline.
Zapier · Delay + Gmail + Filter
5
Automated
Team Notified for Manual Verification
Once documents are in, HR team is notified via email or Slack to begin manual verification. Automation hands off cleanly — no risk of candidates falling through the cracks.
Zapier · Email / Slack Notification
6
Complete
Verification Outcome Communicated
After manual verification, HR sends final outcome email: approved (with joining instructions) or flagged (with follow-up needed). Pipeline record updated accordingly.
Manual step — triggered by HR post-review
Before Automation
~45 min
Per hire — manual email drafting, individual follow-up, tracking submissions across spreadsheet rows, chasing non-respondents by hand.
After Automation
< 5 min
Per hire — form submitted, pipeline runs itself. HR only steps in for actual verification. Zero candidates slip through without a follow-up.
