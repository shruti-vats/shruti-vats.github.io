About

I build people systems that make teams run with less friction. Not just policies and paperwork — actual infrastructure: onboarding flows that don't require hand-holding, hiring pipelines with real structure, dashboards that tell leadership what they need to know.

My background spans two very different environments. At Opt2Deal, I was the first HR hire — building everything from scratch while the team grew from 5 to 50+ people. At Sun Life India, I worked inside a 500+ employee global corporate setup where accuracy and compliance are non-negotiable.

I use automation tools like Zapier and Make to eliminate repetitive work, and AI tools like Claude and ChatGPT to build documentation faster. I document before someone asks. I automate before something becomes a habit.
