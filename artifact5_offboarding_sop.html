Purpose & Scope

This SOP governs the offboarding process for all employee exits — voluntary resignations, contract endings, and involuntary separations. The goal is to ensure data security, payroll accuracy, legal compliance, and a professional exit experience for departing employees — protecting both the company and the individual.
